About the Project
=================

**FoundByMe** was started to solve the problem of "Fragmented Knowledge" in local environments.
Students and researchers often waste time searching for specific information hidden inside PDF slides or lecture notes.

Our Mission
-----------
To create a **Local-First, Privacy-Focused Search Engine** that understands the *context* of your queries, not just the keywords.
